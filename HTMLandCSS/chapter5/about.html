About Yoko's Kitchen

Yoko's Kitchen was founded to share traditional Japanese cooking techniques.

Our mission is to bring authentic flavours and cultural experience to Estonia.

We believe that cooking is not only about food, but also about connection — connection to culture, to history, and to each other. In our classes, students learn how to prepare classic dishes such as sushi, ramen, tempura, and miso soup using traditional methods passed down through generations.

Our workshops are small and interactive, giving every participant the opportunity to practice techniques and ask questions. We focus on fresh ingredients, seasonal products, and beautiful presentation.

Whether you are a beginner or an experienced home cook, Yoko's Kitchen welcomes everyone who wants to discover the beauty of Japanese cuisine in a warm and friendly environment.

Our Philosophy

Fresh ingredients, simple techniques, and respect for tradition.

We value harmony in taste, color, and texture — the essence of Japanese cooking.
