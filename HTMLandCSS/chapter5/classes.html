Our Cooking Classes

We offer traditional Japanese cooking classes for beginners and advanced students.

During our classes, you will learn how to prepare authentic dishes such as sushi, ramen, tempura, gyoza, and miso soup. Each session focuses on mastering essential techniques, knife skills, ingredient selection, and beautiful presentation.

Our small group format ensures personal attention and hands-on practice. All ingredients and equipment are provided — you just need to bring your passion for cooking!

At the end of each class, students enjoy the dishes they prepared together in a friendly and relaxed atmosphere.
